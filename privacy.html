Privacy Policy for Tourly

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tourly. We value your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our travel planning app.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you register for an account, make bookings, or contact us, we collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

Travel Preferences: To provide a personalized experience, we may collect information about your travel preferences, such as destinations, types of accommodations, and activities.

Usage Data: We collect data on how you use our app, including your IP address, device type, operating system, and browsing activity. This helps us improve our services and provide a better user experience.

Location Data: With your permission, we may access your location data to offer location-based services, such as nearby attractions, accommodations, and travel routes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and enhance our travel planning services.
  • Process and manage bookings, including confirmations and modifications.
  • Offer personalized recommendations based on your travel preferences and past bookings.
  • Send you notifications about your bookings, travel updates, and promotional offers (with your consent).
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our app's functionality and user experience.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating our app, processing payments, and delivering services, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Partners: Travel partners such as hotels, airlines, and tour operators to fulfill your bookings and provide travel-related services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights and the rights of our users.

We take appropriate measures to secure your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Your Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You can view and update your personal information through your account settings.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and personal information by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in those emails.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
