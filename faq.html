FAQ - Flight Booking

How can I book a flight?

To book a flight, simply enter your departure and arrival cities, select your travel dates, and choose your preferred flight from the available options. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.

Can I cancel my flight booking?

Yes, you can cancel your flight booking. Please refer to our cancellation policy for details on fees and deadlines. You can cancel your booking through your account or by contacting our customer service.

What if I need to change my flight details?

If you need to change your flight details, you can do so through your account or by contacting our support team. Please note that changes may be subject to availability and additional fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. You can select your preferred payment method during the booking process.

How do I check the status of my flight booking?

You can check the status of your flight booking by logging into your account and viewing your booking history. You will also receive email notifications with updates about your flight.

Do I need to provide a passport number to book a flight?

Generally, you do not need to provide your passport number to book a flight. However, for international flights, you may be required to enter your passport details when completing your booking.

What should I do if I have special requests for my flight?

If you have special requests, such as dietary needs or wheelchair assistance, please contact our customer service team after booking your flight. We will do our best to accommodate your needs.
